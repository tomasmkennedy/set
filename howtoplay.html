How To Play

Rules

  • The cards must all have the same number or have three different numbers.
  • The cards must all have the same shape or have three different shapes.
  • The cards must all have the same shading or have three different shadings.
  • The cards must all have the same color or have three different colors.

Mechanics

12 cards will be layed out 4x3 face up. Select three cards that you believe to be a set and click the "Set!" button. A set is when all cards are the same or all different.

Cards and symbols

There are 81 unique cards. This means there are 1080 unique sets!

The three symbols include: Rectangles, Ovals and Diamonds.

The shapes can be colored solid, shaded or holo.

The shape colors can be blue, green or red.
